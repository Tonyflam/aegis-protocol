## Tone Guidelines

- **Lead with what's working**, not what's coming
- **Never apologize** for the pivot — frame it as "shipping in the right order"
- **Show > tell** — link to the live app, not just promise features
- **Credibility anchors:** hackathon win, test count, LOC count, open source, verified contracts
- **Avoid:** "coming soon" as the main message, overpromising timelines, "trust us"
- **Chinese community:** emphasize 实用 (practical/useful), not just 概念 (concept)
