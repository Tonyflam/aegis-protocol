## STEP 1: CREATE THE COMMUNITY

### How to Create
1. Open X (desktop or mobile)
2. Click **Communities** in the left sidebar (desktop) or bottom nav (mobile)
3. Click **"Create Community"** (top-right or "+" button)
4. If you don't see the option, go directly to: **x.com/i/communities/create**

> **Note:** You must create it from your main account (@uniq_minds or your personal CEO account). The creator becomes the permanent Admin — this cannot be transferred.

### Basic Info to Enter

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Name** | `Aegis Protocol` |
| **Purpose** | `AI-Powered DeFi Protection on BNB Chain` |
| **Description** | See below |
| **Rules** | See below |
| **Visibility** | **Open** (anyone can join — you want growth) |
| **Posting** | **Open** (all members can post — builds engagement) |

## STEP 2: SET COMMUNITY RULES

Add these rules during setup (or edit later under Community Settings → Rules):

**Rule 1: No Financial Advice**
```
This is a tech and product community. Nothing here is financial advice. Do your own research. Don't post buy/sell calls or price predictions as guarantees.
```

**Rule 2: No Spam or Self-Promotion**
```
No shilling other tokens/projects. No referral links. No unsolicited DMs. Repeat offenders get removed.
```

**Rule 3: Be Respectful**
```
No harassment, insults, or personal attacks. Disagree with ideas, not people. Keep it constructive.
```

**Rule 4: Stay On Topic**
```
Posts should be related to Aegis Protocol, $UNIQ, DeFi security, BNB Chain, or AI in crypto. Off-topic posts will be removed.
```

**Rule 5: No Scams or Impersonation**
```
We will NEVER DM you first asking for funds or private keys. Report anyone who does. No fake giveaways or phishing links.
```

## STEP 4: ADD MODERATORS

### Roles in X Communities

| Role | Powers | Who |
|------|--------|-----|
| **Admin** | Full control (settings, rules, remove mods) | You (creator — permanent) |
| **Moderator** | Pin posts, remove posts, mute/ban members | Trusted team members |
| **Member** | Post, reply, like | Everyone who joins |

### How to Add Moderators
1. Go to your Community page
2. Click **"..."** (three dots) → **Community Settings**
3. Go to **Members** tab
4. Find the person → click **"..."** next to their name → **"Make Moderator"**

### Recommended Moderators
- Your personal CEO account (if community is on @uniq_minds)
- 1-2 trusted community members from your Telegram group
- Any team members or advisors

- DM your most active Telegram members to join

## STEP 9: MODERATION BEST PRACTICES

### Daily Tasks
- Check for spam posts and remove them
- Reply to genuine questions within a few hours
- Pin important announcements

### What to Remove Immediately
- Scam/phishing links
- Other token shills
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Fake giveaway posts
- Impersonation of team members

### What to Allow
- Constructive criticism (even negative)
- Price discussion (as long as it's not pump/dump coordination)
- Feature requests and suggestions
- Memes (keeps the community fun)

### Muting vs Banning
- **Mute first** for minor offenses (1 warning)
- **Ban** for scams, phishing, repeated spam, or harassment
- **Never ban** for criticism — respond to it publicly
